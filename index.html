Hi, I’m LaRoyce.

I am a PhD Student at the School of Information Studies at Syracuse University, where I collaborate with the Science of Science + Computational Discovery lab. In December 2020, I graduated from the University of South Florida Schools of Information (BS, Information Studies) and Interdisciplinary Global Studies (BA, Political Science).

My interests are mainly in knowledge representation and reasoning for computational discovery but also in computational argumentation, the Semantic Web, and cognitive science. I am also an active user of R, the statistical programming language, and have authored two packages for it.
